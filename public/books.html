About the Book

A multidisciplinary reference designed to bridge theoretical foundations, high-end computation, and real-world healthcare innovation.

Core Vision

The volume explores how high-performance computing and supercomputing enable AI-driven diagnostics, medical image reconstruction, genomics, drug discovery, epidemiological simulation, healthcare interoperability, digital twin systems, and quantum-enabled medical research.

Who should contribute?

Researchers, academicians, PhD scholars, healthcare technologists, and industry professionals working on advanced computational methods with medical relevance.
